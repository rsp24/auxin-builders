About Auxin Builders

Auxin Builders is one of the most established firms in Kerala, located in Kannur district, Cherupuzha area. We are known for our commitment to construction excellence, sustainability, and green building effects.

Established in 1980, Auxin Builders has over 40+ years of experience as a well-rated developer in Kerala. We take pride in delivering projects that stand the test of time while minimizing environmental impact.
